Project Overview

Pluzon is a fully functional e-commerce website built on Shopify. The goal was to create a user-friendly and visually appealing shopping experience that allows customers to browse and purchase products with ease. The focus was on clear navigation, a smooth checkout process, and a modern design that builds trust with users.

Target Audience

  • Online shoppers looking for quality products at competitive prices.
  • Users who prefer a seamless shopping experience across desktop and mobile devices.

My Role

As the UX/UI Designer, I was responsible for:

  • Customizing the Shopify theme to align with the business goals.
  • Designing a user-friendly layout for optimal navigation and product discovery.
  • Ensuring a smooth checkout experience to reduce cart abandonment.
  • Optimizing mobile responsiveness for a seamless shopping experience.

Challenges & UX/UI Decisions

Biggest Challenges:

  • Ensuring the Shopify theme met both business and user needs.
  • Creating a visually engaging yet functional layout.
  • Improving mobile responsiveness to make sure it works well for all users.

Key UX/UI Decisions:

Navigation

Simplified menu structure for quick product discovery.

Product Display

Clear images and descriptions to help users make informed decisions.

Checkout Process

Streamlined to minimize friction and encourage conversions.

Mobile Optimization

Ensured a responsive design that adapts to different devices.

Final Outcome & Lessons Learned

Pluzon successfully launched and provided a seamless e-commerce experience. Users responded positively to the clean design and ease of navigation.

If I could improve it today:

  • Enhance product filtering options for better usability.
  • Refine typography and spacing for a more modern aesthetic.
  • Try different design layouts and test which one helps more users complete their purchases.
